**Motivation**

For the 5-reaction azo-coupling there are 32 limits or 31 excluding the no-reaction limit.  Blend_fs as described in the 2025 paper and previous posts uses only two of these 31 limits, in combination with each other. For general-purpose application of a closure, it is desirable to consider all of the limits as some may be highly relevant depending on kinetics. This motivates the search for a general approach that in principle can adhere to any of the limits or a combination, depending on the kinetics; and that can be applied to any reaction scheme or system.

**Linear interpolation between nearest limits**

Baldyga's closures (1989, 1994, 2009, 2025) use the average solution concentration to interpolate between the no-reaction limit and infintely fast reaction limit. It seemd posisble that a tiegther bracketing of limits and thereby accurate estimates of C(f) might be obtained by considering all possible limits and then interpolating using the average concentration between the limits that most closely bracketed the average.
